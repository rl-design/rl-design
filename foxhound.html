PROJECT

狐はまだ狩られない
"THIS FOX IS ONE STEP AHEAD OF THE HOUNDS"
(2021)

Graphic Design
Product Design

RELEVANT SOFTWARE

Photoshop
Illustrator

SYNOPSIS

My work reimagining of the iconic series Metal Gear Solid in a cyberpunk near future setting. These illustrations drawn in Photoshop for KawaiiSauce, an apparel brand specializing in Japanese Anime and JDM culture. Graphic and design work was done in both Photoshop and Illustrator for use in merchandise like apparel and computer acessories.

PROCESS - SOCIAL MEDIA CAMPAIGN

When designing the social media slides I used Instagrams slide feature to slowly reveal more and more of the artwork. The viewer would start with a close up of the centerpiece character and finish with the reveal of the mech in the background. The letterbox and subtitle gives the piece a cinematic quality. The video motion graphic was meant as an impactful announcement piece for the upcoming release of the projects merchandise.

PROCESS - LINE ART

The hyper detailed line art for this project was a neccessary labor for this project. I wanted the specific cell shaded aesthetic harkening back to the 90's anime look and feel, a popular and nostalgic art style for our target demographic.

FOO FIGHTERS - SOCIAL MEDIA CAMPAIGN

MAIMED VIPER - SOCIAL MEDIA CAMPAIGN

The social media campaigns for both "FOO FIGHTERS" and "MAIMED VIPER" were meant as an introduction to the artwork used in future merchandise. By creating compelling presentations for each respective artpiece, followers on social media would be introduced and get excited for release of the "drop" aka. limited window to buy merchandise.

PROCESS - SKETCHES AND VISUAL DEVELOPMENT

The character designs for the artwork called for relatable near future technology and imagery. Iconic fashion pieces like the bandana were neccessary for MAIMED VIPER, as the headwear worked as a throughline to the previous iconic character designs by Hideo Kojima.

PROCESS - DESIGN OF MAIMED VIPER

The eventual design of MAIMED VIPER called for her mask to be removed for the cell-shaded version of her artwork. This allowed her eyes to be visable and for the target demographic to better relate and empathize with her as a character.

PROCESS - LOGO DESIGN AND USE IN ARTWORK

The logo designs for this project entailed research ranging from military badges, to previous iterations of the FOXHOUND patch, and even art history - notable with the FOO-FIGHTER's "Kilroy was here" graffiti tag. Corporation and Military Defense logos were designed to add more character to the universe of which the artwork is set in.

PRODUCTION - SLAPS

The final production slaps are printed on holographic material, giving the sticker a oil slick shine.

PRODUCTION - MERCHANDISE

Apparel for the artwork are printed via "film to print" transfer. Apparel design draws inspiration for military fashions and aesthetics with the logo patch printed on the sleeve.
